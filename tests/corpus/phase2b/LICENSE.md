# Provisional corpus permission

The source photographs represented by the DNG derivatives in this directory
were taken by the repository owner's brother. On 2026-07-12 the repository
owner confirmed that the photographer placed no licence restrictions on these
photographs and permitted their use, modification, commitment, CI processing,
and redistribution for Banksia.

This is the project's current rights record. It is intentionally described as
direct photographer permission rather than relabelled as CC0 or another public
licence that the photographer did not explicitly name. A standardized public
test set and a more formal permission record may replace these fixtures later.

The files here are deterministic Bayer-DNG derivatives produced by Banksia from
the permission-covered originals. They contain full sensor mosaics but omit
unneeded proprietary maker-note data.
